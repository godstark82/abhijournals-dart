Abhi International Journal of HR, Finance and Hospitality Management (AIJHRFHM)

The Abhi International Journal of HR,Finance, and Hospitality Management (AIJHRFHM) is a leading peer-reviewed journald edicated to advancing knowledge and research in the fields of human resources,finance, and hospitality management. The journal aims to provide a platform forscholars, industry professionals, and policy makers to share cutting-edgeresearch, innovative strategies, and best practices that address the dynamic challenges faced by these interconnected industries. By fostering aninter disciplinary approach, the journal seeks to contribute to the development of effective management practices and promote the integration of HR, finance, and hospitality sectors to create sustainable growth and organizational success.

With a comprehensive focus, the journal covers a wide range of topics, including human resource management, talent development, organizational behavior, financial analysis, investment strategies, corporate finance, hospitality operations, tourism management, and customer experience management. It also explores emerging trends such as digital transformation in HR and finance, the role of AI in hospitality, and sustainability practices in the service industry.

The journal is committed to publishing high-quality, original research, case studies, theoretical frameworks, and applied research that provide valuable insights for both academia and industry.It aims to address contemporary issues in the HR, finance, and hospitality sectors, promoting the exchange of ideas that help shape the future of the sevital industries.

Through a robust peer-review process and its dedication to academic integrity, the Abhi International Journal of HR,Finance, and Hospitality Management (AIJHM) aspires to be a leading resource for professionals and researchers working to advance practices in these fields while fostering the development of innovative solutions to real-world challenges.

Scope:

The Abhi International Journal of HR,Finance, and Hospitality Management (AIJHRFHM) aims to support a global dialogue onbest practices, research-driven insights, and policy recommendations that drive positive outcomes in these sectors. The journal encourages submissions from both academia and industry professionals, with an emphasis on practical implications that can enhance organizational effectiveness, financial stability, and customer satisfaction. Through its comprehensive scope and commitment to academic rigor, the journal seeks to be a leading resource for advancing knowledge and fostering excellence in HR, finance, and hospitality management.
