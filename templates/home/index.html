About Us

Welcome to Abhi International Journal

Abhi International Journals is a distinguished group of peer-reviewed academic journals dedicated to fostering global knowledge exchange and innovation across diverse domains of research and application. With a commitment to excellence, our journals provide a platform for researchers, academicians, and industry professionals to publish high-quality, original, and impactful research. Covering a wide spectrum of disciplines, including artificial intelligence, medical sciences, engineering, economics, management, and more, Abhi International Journals aims to bridge the gap between theoretical advancements and real-world applications. Our mission is to promote interdisciplinary collaboration and contribute to solving contemporary challenges through cutting-edge research. Guided by an international editorial board comprising renowned experts, we adhere to rigorous peer-review processes, ensuring published content's integrity, relevance, and originality. Dedicated to accessibility, we support open access and strive to make research available to a broader audience worldwide. With a focus on fostering innovation and encouraging scholarly discourse, Abhi International Journals serves as a trusted resource for the global research community.

Our Journals

Our peer-reviewed open-access journals are at the forefront of scholarly publishing, delivering cutting-edge research across a wide range of disciplines. Each journal is committed to providing a platform for high-quality, impactful research that reaches a global audience.

For Authors

Discover our world-class, peer-reviewed open-access journals designed to support researchers at every stage of their journey. Access detailed information about our submission guidelines, editorial policies, and tools to enhance your publication experience. Let us help you disseminate your findings to a global audience.

Librarian Portal

Our Librarian Portal is tailored to meet the needs of institutions and their users. Access flexible subscription options, advanced usage analytics, and seamless integration with library systems to provide the best resources to your community while staying within budget.

For Societies

Partner with us to advance scholarly communication and amplify your society's mission. We offer tailored publishing solutions, exclusive benefits, and unparalleled support to help you achieve your goals. Join a network of like-minded organizations committed to excellence in academic publishing.

For Referees

Join a global network of distinguished experts who help uphold the integrity and quality of our journals. Access comprehensive guidelines, tools, and resources to streamline the peer-review process and contribute to the advancement of scientific knowledge.

ASCI Database

To ensure the highest standards of research integrity, we maintain the ASCI Database—a curated list of eminent reviewers and experts who contribute to the rigorous evaluation of submissions. This trusted network is integral to ensuring impactful and credible publications.

Why Publish with us

Quality

We adhere to a rigorous peer-review process to ensure that every submission meets high academic standards. Our system not only evaluates the quality of the content but also provides constructive feedback to help authors enhance their work.

Flexibility

We embrace diversity in content formats, encouraging authors to present their ideas creatively. From traditional research articles to innovative representations, we support authors in delivering their work in a way that best communicates their findings.

Affordability

Publishing should be accessible to all. We continually strive to make the publishing process straightforward and cost-effective, ensuring affordability for contributors without compromising on quality or reach.

Reach

Publishing with us guarantees exposure to a wide, multidisciplinary audience. Our readers include experts, academics, and professionals from various fields, amplifying the visibility and impact of your work.

Speed

We understand the importance of timely publication. Our streamlined workflows are designed to minimize delays, ensuring that your research reaches the community quickly and efficiently.

Impact

Publishing with us amplifies the reach and relevance of your research. By connecting you with a global community of readers and researchers, we help transform your findings into meaningful contributions that drive progress and innovation across disciplines.
